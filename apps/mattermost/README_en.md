## Introduction

**Mattermost** is an open-source secure team collaboration platform that connects teams, tools, and processes across the entire software development lifecycle. It supports self-hosted deployment and is well suited for organizations that need strong security, compliance, and data control.

## Core Advantages

- **Secure collaboration**: Provides a secure environment for team communication and coordinated work in demanding internal collaboration scenarios.
- **Self-hosted and data controlled**: Deploy on your own infrastructure to retain control over data, access, and system configuration.
- **Workflow integration**: Connect development, operations, and business workflows by bringing messages, tools, and actions into one platform.
- **Rich collaboration capabilities**: Supports messaging, file sharing, calls, and screen sharing for common team collaboration needs.
- **Extensible**: Offers plugins, integrations, and APIs so teams can customize the platform to fit their workflows.
