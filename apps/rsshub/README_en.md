## Product Introduction

**RSSHub** is an open-source, self-hostable universal RSS feed aggregator. It turns the idea of **"Everything is RSSible"** into reality, enabling users to generate standard RSS feeds from thousands of data sources, including social media platforms, news sites, blogs, forums, and more. With a global network of over 5,000 instances, RSSHub delivers millions of pieces of aggregated content to users every day. Its vibrant open-source community continuously contributes new routes, features, and bug fixes to ensure ever-expanding coverage. RSSHub pairs especially well with **Folo**, an AI-powered RSS reader designed for modern feed discovery and reading workflows.

## Key Features

- **Everything is RSSible** – Generate RSS feeds from thousands of websites and platforms, covering social media (Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, etc.), news, blogs, forums, and more
- **Extensive built-in route library** – Huge collection of pre-defined routing rules, continuously maintained and updated by the community
- **Flexible deployment options** – Supports Docker, Vercel, Cloudflare Workers, Nix, Fly.io, and more for fast and easy setup
- **Browser extension support** – Use with **RSSHub Radar** to quickly discover RSS and RSSHub routes supported by the current website
- **Cross-platform ecosystem** – Mobile companion tools available, including **RSSBud** for iOS and **RSSAid** for Android
- **Custom routes** – Users can write and extend their own RSS routing rules as needed
- **Rich community ecosystem** – Comprehensive documentation, Telegram groups and channels, and a curated collection of awesome RSSHub routes
- **Installable as a PWA** – Can be used as a Progressive Web App for a modern reading experience
- **Deep integration with Folo** – Works seamlessly with **Folo**, an AI RSS reader for intelligent feed discovery and reading
- **Open source (AGPL-3.0)** – Fully transparent codebase, supports self-hosting for data privacy and full control
