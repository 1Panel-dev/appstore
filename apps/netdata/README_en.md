## Introduction

**Netdata** is an open-source, real-time infrastructure monitoring platform. It helps you monitor, detect, and act across your entire infrastructure, with per-second metrics and visualizations for servers, containers, and applications.

## Core Advantages

- **Instant Insights**: Access per-second metrics and visualizations to understand infrastructure changes immediately.
- **Zero Configuration**: Deploy quickly without complex setup or heavy initial tuning.
- **ML-Powered**: Detect anomalies, predict issues, and automate analysis with built-in intelligence.
- **Efficient**: Monitor with minimal resource usage while maintaining strong scalability.
- **Secure & Distributed**: Keep data local without depending on centralized collection.
