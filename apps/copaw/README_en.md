## Introduction

**CoPAW** is your personal AI assistant: simple to install, deployable both locally and in the cloud, with multi-end access and easy capability expansion.

## Core Capabilities

- **Reach everywhere** — Connect channels like DingTalk, Feishu, QQ, Discord, and iMessage with one CoPAW instance as needed.
- **You stay in control** — Your memory and personalization remain under your control, locally or in the cloud; scheduled and collaborative tasks can be delivered to designated channels.
- **Skills expansion** — Built-in scheduled tasks and a custom skills directory let CoPAW auto-load capabilities with no lock-in.
